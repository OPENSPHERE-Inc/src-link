# prompt.md test checklist

Checklist template for the test sub-agent in prompt-editor SKILL Step 5 when `.claude/rules/prompt.md` is edited.

## Checklist

1. For each item in the "Meta-explanation patterns to avoid" list, what is the basis on which it is distinguished from the other items?
2. Is it readable from the body (examples and parenthetical notes) that the items do not overlap?
3. When a single sentence contains multiple avoidance patterns, should the whole sentence be deleted, or only the matching portion? Is this policy stated in the body?
4. Are the decision criteria per-item, or shared across the whole list? Is this relationship stated in the body?
5. Give one concrete example that distinguishes "Forward references to other steps" from "Explanatory preambles".
6. State the basis on which "Forward references to other steps" and "Maintenance guidelines aimed at editors" coexist as independent items.
7. If a meta-explanatory passage does not strictly match any of the listed examples, how should it be handled?
